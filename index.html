About Me

Hello! Welcome to my homepage!

My name is Mingfei CAI and I am the first year PhD student from the University of Tokyo in Japan. I am a member of the Human Centered Urban Informatics Laboratory and working with Professor Yoshihide Sekimoto at the Institute of Industrial Science, the University of Tokyo. My research interest is the human mobility semantics extraction and human movement reconstruction.
