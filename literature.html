This is my note page for the papers about the human mobility

Transfer Urban Human Mobility via POI Embedding over Multiple Cities

ACM/IMS Transactions on Data Science, December 2020

embedding reconstruction Secondary Success Danger Warning Info Light Dark

Fusing GPS trajectories data and POI data, the paper proposed image-like embedding with POIs to represent a trajectory like an artificial video, along with transfer learning to exploit data from other cities. It also designed an LSTM-on-CNNs architecture to capture both spatio-temporal and geographical information from citywide human mobility.
